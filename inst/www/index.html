LoL Pick-Ban Optimizer

v0.1

This project is an attempt to optimize the pick-ban process in League of Legends using statistics and machine-learning algorithms. The inputs are the current status of the pick-ban screen, and outputs are champions available for your next pick, ordered by predicted win rate.

Three methods of calculation are provided: Individual (filter by specific champions), Group (filter by groups of champions for larger sample size), and RL (Reinforcement Learning, work in progress).

Pick the color of your team, input one of the following champions names, and click on one of the three buttons to display results.
