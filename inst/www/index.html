LoL Pick-Ban Optimizer

v0.1

This project is an attempt to optimize the pick-ban process in League of Legends using statistics. The inputs are the current status of the pick-ban screen, and outputs are champions available for your next pick, ordered by predicted win rate.

Two methods of calculation are provided: Default and Cluster (group similar champions together for larger sample size).

As an example, type Ryze into Champion 1 under blue team, click the radio button for purple team, and click Optimal Picks. This simluates the situation of a purple team reacting to blue team first picking Ryze. The results indicate that perhaps a mobile/tanky midlaner like Diana or Fizz, or a click-to-win ganker like Vi, would be best in shutting down opponent's midlane. You can then enter Diana for Champion 1 under purple team, then click Optimal Picks again for the second team. The cycle then continues.
